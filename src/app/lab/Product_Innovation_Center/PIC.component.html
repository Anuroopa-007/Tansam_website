Product Innovation Centre

Overview

Tamil Nadu Smart and Advanced Manufacturing Center (TANSAM) is a state-of-the-art facility dedicated to enabling Industry 4.0 adoption and empowering the next generation of innovators. Established as a Siemens Center of Excellence, the center leverages cutting-edge technologies such as Digital Twin, Siemens NX, and Solid Edge CAD to drive product innovation, design optimization, and manufacturing excellence.

Industry 4.0 & Digital Twin: Simulation-driven design and validation. Real-time data integration and virtual commissioning. Smart manufacturing solutions for higher efficiency.

New Product Development (NPD): Concept ideation and prototyping. Rapid design iteration with virtual testing. End-to-end support from concept to production. Support for lightweighting, cost reduction, and performance enhancement in product designs. Product design and modeling using Siemens NX & Solid Edge.

Design Optimization: Finite Element Analysis (FEA), topology optimization, and design validation. Generative design workflows to achieve material and cost efficiency. Integration of sustainability and manufacturability into early design stages.

Startup, MSME & OEM Support: Technology consulting for faster go-to-market. Training and capacity building in digital manufacturing. Access to high-end software and hardware infrastructure.

Engineering Student Engagement: Hands-on exposure to industry-standard tools. Skill development programs aligned with Industry 4.0. Collaboration on academic and industrial projects.

Case Study

A startup in the consumer electronics domain collaborated with the Product Innovation Centre at TANSAM to develop a compact, multifunctional IoT device for smart home automation. The team used Siemens NX to model the enclosure and internal layout, ensuring both aesthetics and manufacturability. FEA simulations helped validate mechanical durability under drop and thermal stress. Parallelly, the PCB layout and circuit design were completed and tested for electromagnetic compatibility (EMC). Iterative virtual prototyping enabled rapid refinements and a functional prototype was delivered in under 6 weeks. This streamlined workflow reduced product development time by 50% and ensured the design met both performance and cost objectives—demonstrating TANSAM’s capability in accelerating product innovation.
