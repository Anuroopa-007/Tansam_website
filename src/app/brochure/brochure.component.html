Our Mobile App and Web Development Process

PHASE A

A1 Specifications & Planning

  • Solidify what is being built so we are aligned.
  • Research libraries and services for integration.
  • Decide on coding language architecture and unique elements.

A2 Designs, Wireframe & Prototype

Designs for appearance and functionality (and beauty).

A3 Estimates & Timeline

Spreadsheet outlining all tasks involved, timeline and total cost.

PHASE B

B1 Build

Engineers build the software, fix bugs, and improve scalability.

B2 Test

QA team tests the build, identifying bugs and required improvements.

B3 Deploy

Release on app stores or live websites.

B4 Measure

Our product team measures the progress & effectiveness of the latest build.

B5 Maintain

Should we exit the software development loop, we have lightweight plans available for continued, long-term maintenance.
