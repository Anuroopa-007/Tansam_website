Product Innovation Centre

Overview

Tamil Nadu Smart and Advanced Manufacturing Center (TANSAM) is a state-of-the-art facility dedicated to enabling Industry 4.0 adoption and empowering the next generation of innovators. Established as a Siemens Center of Excellence, the center leverages cutting-edge technologies such as Digital Twin, Siemens NX, and Solid Edge CAD to drive product innovation, design optimization, and manufacturing excellence. Industry 4.0 & Digital Twin: Simulation-driven design and validation. Real-time data integration and virtual commissioning. Smart manufacturing solutions for higher efficiency. New Product Development (NPD): Concept ideation and prototyping. Rapid design iteration with virtual testing. End-to-end support from concept to production. Support for lightweighting, cost reduction, and performance enhancement in product designs. Product design and modeling using Siemens NX & Solid Edge. Design Optimization: Finite Element Analysis (FEA), topology optimization, and design validation. Generative design workflows to achieve material and cost efficiency. Integration of sustainability and manufacturability into early design stages. Startup, MSME & OEM Support: Technology consulting for faster go-to-market. Training and capacity building in digital manufacturing. Access to high-end software and hardware infrastructure. Engineering Student Engagement: Hands-on exposure to industry-standard tools. Skill development programs aligned with Industry 4.0. Collaboration on academic and industrial projects.
