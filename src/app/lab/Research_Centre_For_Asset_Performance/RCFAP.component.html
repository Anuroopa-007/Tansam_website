Research Centre For Asset Performance

Overview

The Center for Predictive Engineering Analytics (CPEA) Lab specializes in digital twin development and advanced engineering simulations to enhance product design, reduce prototyping costs, and accelerate innovation. By integrating technologies like FEA, CFD, MBD, and thermal-structural analysis, the lab provides predictive insights across automotive, aerospace, energy, and manufacturing sectors. CPEA collaborates with industries, researchers, and academic institutions to solve real-world engineering challenges through simulation-driven solutions.

Case Study

A large-scale manufacturing plant faced frequent unplanned downtimes due to failures in critical rotating machinery, impacting productivity and increasing maintenance costs. The CPEA Lab partnered with the facility to deploy a predictive maintenance solution using digital twin modeling, sensor-based data acquisition, and advanced analytics. Real-time data from IoT-enabled vibration and temperature sensors were streamed via MQTT and analyzed using Python and Grafana dashboards. Through thermal-structural simulations and pattern recognition algorithms, the team identified early signs of wear and optimized the maintenance schedule. This solution reduced unplanned downtimes by 40%, improved asset lifespan, and delivered an annual cost savings of ₹18 lakhs—demonstrating the value of integrating simulation and Industrial IoT in asset performance management.
